Sound good?

Save money, save the planet, improve your home, and provide work for local installers… what do you have to lose?

This is a unique and exciting opportunity to electrify our homes and community. To get the pilot off the ground, we need households willing to participate.

The proposal

Rewiring Australia wants to subsidise and support a significant sample of households in a community to go electric in six important ways. We think Canberra could be that community!

There are other communities in contention. With Canberra’s natural advantages, we think we can do it better and first. And in this competition everyone wins.

Of course, we need some idea as to the scale of the task. It may be that you have already done much to decarbonise your home.

It may be that you have already had some help from various government initiatives and have solar cells installed etc etc. Perhaps not. Perhaps you rent. Perhaps your circumstances have never allowed you to do any of this.

So, we would like to know something of your needs, your homes, and how far you have come in your own efforts (towards domestic decarbonisation)

via a short Survey. 1 We will keep you updated.
